    Rebuttal:

    Nangeli first appeared in a fictional story by C. Radhakrishnan, published in The Pioneer on March 8, 2007. Characters like Nangeli and her husband Kadappan were fictional creations.

    The story gained traction in 2009 via blogger Michael David, followed by outlets like The Hindu and BBC in 2016.
